Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 14, 2026

Morning Proof ("we", "our", or "the app") is a habit tracking app that helps you build morning routines through photo verification and accountability. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

Summary: Your data stays on your device. Photos are processed for verification but never stored. We don't sell your data or show you ads.

Information We Collect

Account Information

When you sign in with Apple or Google, we may receive:

  • Email address (optional, based on your sharing preferences)
  • Name (optional, based on your sharing preferences)
  • User identifier (a unique ID from Apple or Google)

You can also use the app without signing in.

Photos

When you verify habits like making your bed:

  • Photos are sent to our AI service (Anthropic's Claude) for verification
  • Photos are processed immediately and not stored on any server
  • Only the verification result (score and feedback) is saved locally on your device

Health Data

With your permission, we access Apple Health to automatically track:

  • Step count - to verify morning movement goals
  • Sleep data - to track sleep duration and quality
  • Workout data - to detect morning exercise

This data stays on your device and is never sent to external servers.

Screen Time

If you enable app blocking, we use Apple's Family Controls to:

  • Block selected apps until you complete your morning routine
  • Store your app selection preferences locally

We cannot see which specific apps you block - Apple's privacy protections prevent this.

Usage Data

We store locally on your device:

  • Habit completion history and streaks
  • App settings and preferences
  • Achievement progress
  • Journal entries and notes you create

How We Use Your Information

  • Verify habit completion through AI photo analysis
  • Track your streaks and progress
  • Send you reminder notifications (with your permission)
  • Improve app stability through crash reports

Third-Party Services

Anthropic (Claude AI)

Photos are sent to Anthropic's API for bed verification. Anthropic processes images according to their privacy policy. Images are not stored after processing.

Firebase Crashlytics

We may collect anonymous crash reports to improve app stability. This includes technical information about crashes but no personal data.

Apple & Google Sign-In

Authentication is handled by Apple and Google according to their respective privacy policies.

Data Storage & Security

  • All personal data is stored locally on your device
  • We do not maintain servers that store your personal information
  • Photos are transmitted securely (HTTPS) for verification
  • You can delete all app data by uninstalling the app

Your Rights

You can:

  • Access all your data within the app
  • Delete your data by uninstalling the app
  • Revoke health data permissions in iOS Settings
  • Disable notifications at any time
  • Sign out or use the app anonymously

Children's Privacy

Morning Proof is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes through the app or by updating the "Last Updated" date.
